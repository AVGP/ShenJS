About ShenJS

JSConf China is a two day conference focused on JavaScript and Node.js technologies. This developer driven event brings together notable figures from both the Chinese and international JavaScript communities to share their knowledge and passion for JavaScript. After Shanghai, Beijing and Hangzhou, JSConf China is going to Shenzhen in the summer 2015.
